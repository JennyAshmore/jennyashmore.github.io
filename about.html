I'm Jenny Ashmore and I'm passionate about photography in all it's forms; from capturing those candid teary-eyed happy wedding moments to high gloss saturated studio perfection.

Life is for living and I've lived all over the world. North West England is now home but I love travelling for work or pleasure so feel free to get in touch for new projects and collaborations.

Jenny Ashmore's Photography History

  • 2018 - The new version of my website and portfolio was published, along with my first ever photography book.

  • 2016 - I was offered and snapped up a position studying photography for two years. I was well on my way to achieve a Higher National Diploma qualification in Photography.

  • 2013 - I started my professional photography career by launching Ashmore Photography, to fantastic success!

  • 2009 - This was the year I took my first photography course with international photographer: Jim Zukerman, and knew that I was hooked.
